-About Us-

Since opening our doors in 2009, we have been consistently recognized for our ability to bring a customized, tailored planning experience to clients who dream of an approachable, detail-rich, luxury wedding celebration.We approach every wedding with a detailed design and planning process that ensures you have the time and space to dream, and to build a team that you trust to execute the many ideas we will create together.

We love making this journey a personalized one. Not only through visual touches, but through building a team of professionals that will absolutely blow you away throughout your planning and especially on your wedding day. We care about your ideas, your worries, and your priorities, and manage them all so your family and closest friends experience a day that is perfectly yours.
