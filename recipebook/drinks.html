-Cola-

Succulent pieces of marinated chicken, charred to perfection for a smoky and flavorful Indian delight.

-Ingredients-

For the Marinade

  • 500 grams prawn
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • salt to taste

For the Tikka Skewers

  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes)
  • Cooking oil for brushing

-Process-

STEP 1 : Marinating prawn

In a bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon
juice, and salt. Add the prawn pieces to the marinade and mix well, ensuring each piece is coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

STEP 2 : Preparing the Tikka Skewers

Preheat your grill or oven to high heat (around 200°C or 400°F). If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning and then thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.

STEP 3 : Cooking the prawn Tikka

In a blender, combine the soaked cashew nuts and water. Blend until you have a smooth cashew paste. Set aside. Brush the chicken skewers with a little cooking oil to prevent sticking to the grill or oven rack. Place the skewers on the grill or in the oven and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a charred, slightly crispy exterior.

STEP 4 : Serving Chicken Tikka

In a blender, combine the soaked cashew nuts and water. Blend until you have a smooth cashew paste. Set aside. Remove the chicken tikka skewers from the grill or oven and let them rest for a few minutes.Serve the chicken tikka hot with slices of lemon and garnish with fresh cilantro or mint leaves. It pairs wonderfully with naan, rice, or a side salad.

-Orange Juice-

Tender marinated chicken wrapped in warm flatbreads, a delightful fusion of Indian flavors in every bite.

-Ingredients-

For the Marinade

  • 500 grams boneless chicken (cut into tin strips)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • salt to taste

For the filling

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (thinly sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped, for garnish)
  • Salt to taste

For Assembling the Kathi Rolls

  • Roti or paratha (flatbreads, you can use
    store-bought or homemade)
  • Mint chutney or yogurt-based dressing
  • Sliced cucumbers and tomatoes (optional)

-Process-

STEP 1 : Marinating the Chicken

Combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala,salt and coat the chicken evenly.
Cover and let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

STEP 2 : Preparing the Filling

Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle. Add sliced onions and bell peppers to the pan.Sauté until they become tender and slightly caramelized. Season with salt to taste.

STEP 3 :Cooking the Marinated Chicken

In a blender, combine the soaked cashew nuts and water. Blend until you have a smooth cashew paste. Set aside. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Add a little oil to prevent sticking. Place the marinated chicken strips on the pan and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

STEP 4 :Assembling the Kathi Rolls

Warm the roti or paratha in a dry skillet or on a griddle.Place a spoonful of the cooked chicken in the center of each roti.Top the chicken with the sautéed onion and bell pepper mixture. Drizzle mint chutney or yogurt-based dressing over the filling.Optionally, add sliced cucumbers and tomatoes for freshness. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.Roll up the roti to form a wrap or roll, securing it with parchment paper or foil if needed.

Serve your homemade Chicken Kathi Rolls as a delicious and flavorful Indian street food-style dish!

-Oreo Shake-

Golden rings of onion perfection, fried to a delightful crunch and served with a zesty dipping sauce.

-Ingredients-

For the Onion Rings

  • 2 large onions, cut into rings
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust for spice)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Cooking oil for frying

For the Dipping Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

-Process-

STEP 1 : Preparing the Onion Rings

Peel and slice the onions into rings, separating them.

STEP 2 : Coating the Onion Rings

In a bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
Mix well to create a seasoned flour mixture.Dip each onion ring into the buttermilk, allowing any excess to
drip off. Coat the onion rings in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring they are evenly covered.Place the
coated onion rings on a baking sheet or wire rack.

STEP 3 : Frying the Onion Rings

Heat cooking oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C). Carefully add the coated onion rings to the hot oil in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the fryer or pot. Fry the onion rings until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per batch. Use a slotted spoon to remove them and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, ketchup, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper to create a flavorful dipping sauce.

Enjoy your homemade Crispy Onion Rings as a delicious and satisfying snack!

A popular Indian dish known for its smoky and flavorful taste.

-Ingredients-

For the Samosa Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (for kneading)

    • For the spice blend

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled, peeled, and chopped into small cubes
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil for frying

-Process-

STEP 1 : Making the Samosa Dough

In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, and vegetable oil (or ghee). Gradually add water, little by little, and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and pliable. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

STEP 2 : Preparing the Samosa Filling

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent. Stir in minced ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté for a couple of minutes until fragrant.Add chopped carrots and green peas. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften. Add the boiled and chopped potatoes to the pan.Sprinkle in the ground coriander, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes, ensuring the spices coat the vegetables.

STEP 3 : Shaping and Filling the Samosas

Divide the rested dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball. Roll out each ball into a thin oval or circle.Cut the circle in half to form two semi-circles.Fold each semi-circle in half to create a cone shape. Seal the edges with a little water to form a triangle pocket. Fill each samosa pocket with the cooled vegetable mixture.

STEP 4 : Cook the Chicken

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Carefully slide the filled samosas into the hot oil. Fry the samosas until they turn golden brown and crisp, turning them occasionally for even frying. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes. Remove the fried samosas from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to remove excess oil.

Enjoy your homemade Vegetable Samosas with their flavorful and savory filling encased in crispy golden pastry!
